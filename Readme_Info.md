Segment -> I - Project Idea
The project aims to predict the exam marks of students in a batch of 40 based on multiple academic and behavioral factors. These include:
•	Current performance and previous academic records
•	Attendance consistency
•	Quality of notes and study materials
•	Assignment & project completion quality
•	Class participation
•	Exam-taking skills (time management, anxiety, strategy)
•	Cognitive and learning factors
•	Study environment and psychological factors
The final output is to provide insights and personalized recommendations that help each student excel in their career and achieve their best potential.
